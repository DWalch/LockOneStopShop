Two Target Cleave
Opening Rotation
The following is using this spec: DC/ID/HoD/GoServ/DB or Darkglare. The reason it's between Demonbolt and Darkglare is because they are both viable depending on the situation; if there is a single priority target (such as the boss) that needs to be killed but there is also another target that is cleaved lightly, take Demonbolt. If the two targets are equally as important to kill take Darkglare.
  • [Prepull] Demonic Empowerment - 3 Soul Shards
  • [Prepull] Demonbolt - 4 Soul Shards
  • Grimoire Service: Felguard - 3 Soul Shards
  • Doomguard - 2 Soul Shards
  • Hand of Gul'dan - 0 Soul Shards
  • Demonic Empowerment
  • Demonbolt - 1 Soul Shards
  • Demonbolt - 2 Soul Shards
  • Demonbolt - 3 Soul Shards
  • Call Dreadstalkers - 1 Soul Shards
  • Summon Darkglare - 0 Soul Shards - If talented
  • Demonic Empowerment
  • Thal'kiel's Consumption
  • Resume priority rotation
General Rotation
  • Keep Doom up
  • Keep your Demons buffed with Demonic Empowerment
  • Summon Doomguard on cooldown
  • Summon Service: Felguard on cooldown
  • Summon Darkglare on cooldown
  • Call Dreadstalkers on cooldown
  • Cast Hand of Gul'dan when possible (4 Soul Shards)
  • Cast Thal'kiel's Consumption with at least 6 Wild Imps and 2 Dreadstalkers + Doomguard/Darkglare/GoServ: Felguard if possible
  • Fill with Demonbolt
General Thal'kiel's Consumption usage: 2 Dreadstalkers, 6 Wild Imps - If possible, slide in Summon Darkglare as well if talented since it's a short CD that usually lines up with Thal'kiel's Consumption.
It is best to combo your Demon summons in sets of 2 if you can. For example: Doomguard + Hand of Gul'dan, Hand of Gul'dan + Call Dreadstalkers or Doomguard + Call Dreadstalkers.
Three+ Target Cleave
Opening Rotation
The following is using this spec: DC/ID/HoD/GoServ or GoSyn/Darkglare. This rotation assumes you will have three or more targets to cleave consistently, if at some point there is a lapse of that situation (three or more targets) swap your generator from Demonwrath to Shadow Bolt.
  • [Prepull] Demonic Empowerment - 3 Soul Shards
  • [Prepull] Shadow Bolt - 4 Soul Shards
  • Grimoire Service: Felguard - 3 Soul Shards - If talented
  • Doomguard - 2 Soul Shards
  • Hand of Gul'dan - 0 Soul Shards
  • Demonic Empowerment
  • Demonwrath - to at least 3 Soul Shards
  • Call Dreadstalkers - 1 Soul Shards
  • Summon Darkglare - 0 Soul Shards
  • Demonic Empowerment
  • Thal'kiel's Consumption
  • Resume priority rotation
General Rotation
  • Keep Doom up
  • Keep your Demons buffed with Demonic Empowerment
  • Summon Doomguard on cooldown
  • Summon Service: Felguard on cooldown - If talented
  • Summon Darkglare on cooldown
  • Call Dreadstalkers on cooldown
  • Cast Hand of Gul'dan when possible (4 Soul Shards)
  • Cast Thal'kiel's Consumption with at least 6 Wild Imps and 2 Dreadstalkers + Doomguard/Darkglare/GoServ: Felguard if possible
  • Fill with Demonwrath or Shadow Bolt
General Thal'kiel's Consumption usage: 2 Dreadstalkers, 6 Wild Imps - If possible, slide in Summon Darkglare as well if talented since it's a short CD that usually lines up with Thal'kiel's Consumption.
It is best to combo your Demon summons in sets of 2 if you can. For example: Doomguard + Hand of Gul'dan, Hand of Gul'dan + Call Dreadstalkers or Doomguard + Call Dreadstalkers.
