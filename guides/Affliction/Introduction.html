The Affliction warlock has gone through some pretty drastic changes from through legion, with 7.0 -> 7.1.0 being entirely based around our dots, moving from a drain / tunnel spec with 7.1.5 and 7.2.0, and now to a playstyle that allows for both to happen, allowing a vast amount of flexibility and adaptation with the new TOS fights.

Affliction's Strengths:

  • Extremely strong spread damage
  • Single target damage that scales off of adds with Wrath of Consumption
  • Extremely strong multi target that rivals even the master of cleave, Destruction
  • Incredible mobility with a large swath of our damage coming from instant cast dots.

Affliction's Weaknesses:

  • Best damage reduces mobility duraing a fight to a minimum
  • Very long ramp-up time and poor burst damage.
  • Some RNG aspects involved with Tormented Soul can be very frustrating on single target
  • Reliant on multiple targets to get the most out of single target
Post 7.2.5 Hotfixes
  • Damage of all abilities reduced by 5%.
7.2.5 Changes

Legendaries

  • The Master Harvester have been added
  • Soul of the Netherlord have been added.

Artifact

  • Wrath of Consumption has been nurfed from 2% per stack to 1% per stack.

Talents

  • Soul Effigy has been removed.
  • Death's Embrace has replaced Effigy as the level 100 talent. It is a ramping execute, providing 0% bonus damage when the target is at 35%, and 50% bonus damage when the target hits 0%.
  • Siphon Life and Phantom Singularity have swapped places. Siphon Life also recieved the following changes:
    • Damage increased by 30%
    • Healing recieved down from 100% to 60% of damage.
  • Phantom Singularity has major changes:
    • cooldown dropped from 60 to 40 seconds
    • damage increased by 5%
    • The healing done has been reduced from 30% to 20% damage dealt
  • Contagion's effect has been reduced from 18% to 15%.
  • Malefic Grasp has had its effect reduced from 70% to 25%
  • Haunt has recieved multiple changes
    • Cooldown reduced from 30 to 25.
    • Damage reduced from 805% spelldamage to 500%.
    • Damage Amplifier reduced from 30% to 15%.
    • Duration reduced from 15 seconds to 10 seconds.
  • Writhe in Agony reduced from 20 max stacks to 15 max stacks
  • Soul Harvest recieved multiple changes
    • Base duration reduced from 15 seconds to 12 seconds.
    • Duration per Agony increased from 2 to 4 added seconds.

Spells

  • All warlock spell damage increased by 20% The following spells have recieved a nurf to leave them net-unneffected by this change:
    • Soul Flame
    • Seed of Corruption
