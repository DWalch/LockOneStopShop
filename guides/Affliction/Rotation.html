The rotation for affliction has changed drastically in 7.2.5, simply because more talents are now viable. They focus on our 3 abilities in tier 15: Haunt, Writhe in Agony:, and Malefic Grasp

I am trying to keep the formats for the 3 specs similar, going with Opener, General Rotation, and Cooldown notes / other comments.

Malefic Grasp

Malefic Grasp used to use the Drain / Maintenance cycles, but these are relitively gone now in 7.2.5. They exist somewhat, but are much less important / relevent due to the drop in shards with the departure of Effigy.

Strengths

  • Pure Single Target
  • Any time there are damage amps needed
  • Any time there is an execute phase, even on 2 target fights.

Opener

The MG Opener is pretty similar to the 7.2.0 opener

  1. Prepot at 1 (Or what ever count is safe with your trigger happy guild)
  2. Begin with Agony: -> Siphon Life -> Corruption:
  3. Cast one Unstable Affliction:then drain until your Corruption: is at about 7 seconds left.
  4. Begin Re-dotting again with Agony: -> SL -> Corruption:.
  5. At this point you should be at 4 shards, minimum of 3 souls, and you can begin your standard rotation.

Standard Rotation

Malefic Grasp uses a pretty standard rotation that is similar to its 7.2.0, with a key difference in that Unstable Affliction:has lost a significant amount of bite, while other dots have gained a significant amount of damage. From this point its a priority system that will have some eb and flow similar to the "Drain / Maintence Cycles" We had in 7.2.0, but much less extreme.

  1. Always line drain with at least one Unstable Affliction:to stack both the MG and Contagion debuff.
  2. Do not let your 3 prime dots drop on your main target
  3. Always maintain at least Agony: and Corruption: on off targets for the bonus shards from them (2piece tier 20 gives the Corruption: shards)
  4. Always line up multiple Unstable Affliction: with large damage amps like Soul Harvest, Wrath of Consumption. Make sure to Reap and Drain Soul: as much as possible during these times.
  5. Always redot in Agony: -> Siphon Life -> Corruption:.
  6. Like before, try to avoid casting a Unstable Affliction:without being able to Drain Soul: during it, to maximize that 25% damage increase on it.

Cooldowns Other notes

  • When cooldowns are about to be used, its very important to have a 4stock of shards and fresh dots on the target to maximize the burst from the cooldowns.
  • The 2piece Tier 20 will periodically give you a flood of shards becuase its on the RPPM system. Keep that in mind while you're playing to try to stay below 3 shards when going into Unstable Affliction: casts.
  • Malefic Grasp is best if you can maintain Drain Soul: time over 50%. Significant movement may lean itself towards Haunt instead of Malefic Grasp.
  • Be sure to time your Life Tap for movement to avoid losing Drain Soul: down time.
  • This rotation is the most mana intensive, so keep that in mind.
  • Malefic Grasp is also the most GCD Locked rotation of the 3, so tap mobs for Souls / Wrath smartly.

Writhe in Agony:

While the talent itself has gotten less strong in comparison to the base Agony:, with the flat 20% damage buff to all affliction dots, Agony: should hit roughlythe same while this spec. The playstyle has had little changes since 7.2.0 as well, but has much less single target capabilities with the loss of effigy.

Strengths

  1. 3 Target +fights
  2. High mobility fights.

Opener

  1. Pre-pot and Agony: -> Siphon Life -> Corruption: in that order.
  2. Dot any other targets.
  3. Drain Soul: until shard 4 is generated.
  4. Unstable Affliction:and continue draining until Corruption: is at 7 seconds left. Sometimes this will happen without the ability to Drain Soul:, in which case just go to the next step.
  5. Redot in an Agony: -> Siphon Life -> Corruption: order. Redot any other targets as well.

General Rotation

From here, the Writhe in Agony: should be very similar to the 7.2.0 writhe without the extra Effigy to dot. Try to keep this priority in mind.

  1. Do not hit 5 shards ever. If you hit 4 shards, try to get an Unstable Affliction: out as soon as possible
  2. Don't let your dots drop in the following priority, 1 being the highest priority dot.
    1. Agony:
    2. Corruption:
    3. Siphon Life
    4. Unstable Affliction:.
  3. Drain Soul: whenever possible

Cooldowns Other notes

  • Cooldowns have much less impact on Writhe due to the lack of extra amps on all dots.
  • Be sure to keep contagion applied through heroism and other cooldowns on your priority target.
  • Drain Sniping is exceptionally strong with a Writhe in Agony: build due to the free GCDs that you have from not having to maintain Malefic Grasp on a target.
  • This spec is exceptionally strong on Council fights, as well as any fight where you need to maintain high boss damage as well as keeping damage on steady streams of adds. It will lose effectiveness if the targets die within 20 seconds compared to Malefic Grasp and Haunt.
  • This spec is the least mana intensive, so just life tap smartly.

Haunt

The biggest change for 7.2.5, is that Haunt is no longer just this meme that some people like to think is good, but it actually has a place in the raid environment. As such, we will spend a few moments to talk about how to play with haunt.

Strengths

  • Hecktic Add fights such as Botanist Mythic where you can constantly refresh Haunt. Ideal situation is a target living between 10 and 15 seconds.
  • High movement single / two target fight.

Opener

  1. Prepot -> Agony: -> Siphon Life -> Corruption: any available targets.
  2. If no adds that will need haunt are spawning in the first 25 seconds.
    1. On the boss: Haunt -> Unstable Affliction: -> Unstable Affliction: -> Drain Soul:.
    2. Begin Dot maintance.
    Otherwise:
    1. Drain Soul: until 4 shards
    2. Unstable Affliciton once
    3. Drain Soul: again until 7 seconds left on Corruption:.
    4. Begin Re-dotting

At this point there is two different priorities, With adds and without adds.

Rotation Without Adds

Note: This should only be happening in things like Mythic Plus (If you ran haunt) or on down times during a phase that has minimal adds.

  1. Prioritize Dot maintenance similar to WiA
  2. Try to pool shards while haunt is down
  3. At about 5 seconds left on the haunt Cooldown, refresh dots to get max duration on your boss.
  4. When haunt is about 2 seconds from being off cd, you want to be at 4+ shards with 3+ souls.
  5. At that point (Haunt is 2 seconds from being off cooldown) cast: Unstable Affliciton -> Haunt + Reap -> Unstable Affliction: -> Unstable Affliction: -> Unstable Affliction: -> Drain Soul:

Rotation With Adds

  1. Maintain dots on your primary boss target
  2. Maintain at least Corruption: / Agony: on all adds
  3. When a priority target to die is found, make sure to refresh dots to last for the 8-10 seconds it takes to kill the target
  4. Haunt -> Unstable Affliction: x3 is the typical order then drain. This will net you down only 1 shard, which you Should get back from just passive regen.
  5. If you have a high amount of shards / dots out for shards, use a Unstable Affliction: before haunt just for added damage.
  6. Do not shard cap. If waiting for a priority target, Unstable Affliction:the boss target.

Cooldowns Other notes

  • Line up cooldowns to be up during a specific target that needs to die, maximizing the damage amps under the Haunt debuff.
  • Haunt amplifies soul flame, so if you're in Night Hold cheesing Scorp, you can use it to add some extra Soul Flame damage into the boss.
  • Haunt is a middle ground for mana use, but not nearly as bad as Malefic Grasp simply because you aren't going to be tunneling the boss.

Conduit vs Siphon Life vs Demonic Embrace

With 7.2.5 there has been a huge change up for the tier 100 tree. Here is their strengths and weaknessess

  • Demonic Embrace
    • Strengths
      • "Last Phase Enrage" bosses such as Star Augur
      • Pulls ahead on fights that last longer.
    • Weaknesses
      • Low damage outside of "Last Phase"
      • Even worse damage if you hero in phase 1.
  • Siphon Life
    • Strengths
      • Consistant damage through the whole fight.
      • Very strong self healing.
    • Weaknesses
      • Extra GCDs through the fight that drop Unstable Affliction: / burst damage.
  • Soul Conduit
    • Strengths
      • Highest "Possible parses" if good RNG
      • Allows Malefic Grasp to play very similar to 7.2.0
    • Weaknesses
      • Lowest "Average" damage of the 3
      • RNG will lead it to be a very poor choice for progression

Cross Spec Notes

This is just a few final notes for all 3 specs to keep in mind.

  1. You should always be running Grimoire of Supremacy and Doomguard, unless you need the stun, then run Infernal.
  2. Phantom Singularity is about .5% ahead of Soul harvest, but due to the nature of fights, its likely that Soul Harvest will be much better for progression due to it amplyfing damage to get out of a specific phase earlier.
