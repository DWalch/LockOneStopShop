What does this stat do?

Haste - Haste is your strongest stat as a Destruction Warlock. Haste makes everything faster, most importantly reducing the cooldown on conflagrate.

Crit - Incinerate and Immolate both generate more shards from critical strikes. Also your Critical Strike makes your Chaos Bolt hit harder as this spell is a guaranteed crit already.

Versatility - A weaker, but entirely passive, version of mastery.

Mastery - Passively increases your damage and reduces your damage taken, in addition to a random component. In 7.2.5 they made mastery considerably less RNG, most of it's value is now passive..

Obviously the precise stat weights are gonna vary a lot depending on your gear, talents and obviously the fight itself. This segment of the guide is just made to give you a general idea of what stats you should be aiming for.

Haste - 1.4 Mastery - 1.2 Crit - 1.2 Vers - 1.1 Int - 1.0
