(2 Pieces): Incinerate has a 100% chance to generate 1 additional Soul Shard Fragment.
(4 Pieces): Chaos Bolt always gains maximum benefit from your Mastery: Chaotic Energies.

Legendaries

Destruction only legendaries:

Feretory of Souls
- Fire spells have a 10% chance to generate a Soul Shard.
Odr, Shawl of the Ymirjar
- Your Havoc target takes an additional 8% damage from your single target spells.
Alythess's Pyrogenics
- Enemies affected by your Rain of Fire take an additional 10% damage from your fire spells.
Lessons of Space-Time
- When you cast Dimensional Rift you gain a buff that increases your damage dealt by 10% for as long as the rift is open.
Magistrike Restraints
- Your Chaos Bolts have a 20% chance to cleave onto an additional target within 30 yards of your Chaos Bolt Target. This can also proc off your Havoc Target, but it can only proc once per cast.
Warlock only legendaries:
Pillars of the Dark Portal
- The cast time of your Demonic Gateway is removed and you're able to use the gateway twice before it goes on cooldown. When wearing this legendary your Demonic Gateway is also placed a lot faster making you able to use it as soon as it's been cast where it takes a few seconds after placing it before you can use it without wearing this legendary.
Other legendaries:
Prydaz, Xavaric's Magnum Opus
- Every 30 seconds you gain a shield for 25% of your max health for 30 seconds.
Celumbra, the Night's Dichotomy (crafted)
- When an enemy is within 12 yards of you, you take 5% reduced damage and have 10% increased movement speed. When no enemies are within 30 yards of you, you heal for 1% of your max hp every second.
Norgannon's Foresight
- When standing still for 6 seconds you gain a buff. When you move you're able to cast while moving for 4 seconds.
Sephuz's Secret
- Passively grants 10% movement speed and 2% haste. When cc'ing, dispelling or interrupting something this effect is increased to 70% movement speed and 25% haste. This effect has a 30 second cooldown.
Kil'jaeden's Burning Wish
- An on-use trinket that deals ~800k damage to your target and all enemies within 10 yards of that target on a 1 minute 15 seconds cooldown.
Sin'dorei Spite DEMONOLOGY LEGENDARY
- When summoning either your Infernal or Doomguard you gain a 25 second buff that increases your damage done by 15%.

Final Thoughts

With the recent legendary balancing done in patch 7.2 a lot of legendaries that before weren't competitive are now a lot better.

  • Sin'dorei Spite + Lessons of Space-Time is the optimal legendary combination for single target.
  • Lessons of Space-Time + Magistrike Restraints is the best legendary combination for cleave.
The most important legendary as Destruction is Lessons of Space-Time and this should be used in all situations.
For a ranking of how legendaries perform go look under the "Sims" section.

Trinkets

  • Whispers in the Dark is a proc trinket that increases your haste for 12 seconds, once this expires your haste is decreased for 8 seconds. This trinket is extremely strong as your Soul Shard generation increases drastically when up. This trinkets value increases a lot on more than 1 target.
  • Erratic Metronome makes your spells have a chance to increase your haste, this effect stacks but stacking it does not refresh the buff. Essentially this works a bit like a stat stick intellect + haste trinket.
  • Brinewater Slime in a Bottle is also a very strong option if able to get a good version. Obviously other stat stick trinkets have the same value as this, but it may be easier obtaining a higher item level trinket from Helya than from a world quest.
