While there are many addons out there, there are certainly a few that stick out as great ones, here are some that I highly recommend:
  • Weak Auras 2
    The quintessential addon for tracking anything and everything there is in World of Warcraft. There are many places that you can find Weak Auras including:
    • wago.io, specifically my auras can be found here: https://wago.io/p/Loozy.
    • Not's website
    • Others on MMO-Champion
    • Doom Shards
      This version of Doomshards has been modified to work in tandem with the Soul Shard bit change that comes into effect in 7.2.5. Doom Shards is an addon that allows you to track your Soul Shards like normal but has the added functionality of being able to predict/tell you when you will gain your next Soul Shard to avoid over capping.
