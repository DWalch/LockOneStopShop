Rotation/Priority List

Single Target Rotation/Priority

The Affliction Warlock has two different phases for its single target rotation: Maintenance and Drain cycles. These also rely on being specced into Malefic Grasp.

Maintenance

  1. Cast Life Tap If you are below 30% mana at the start (before any dot refreshes)
  2. Maintain Agony on all available targets.
  3. Maintain Corruption on boss and longer lived targets. Use Seed of Corruption if it will save GCDs.
  4. Maintain Siphon Life if specced into it onto longer lived targets.

Malefic Grasp Drain Cycle

  1. Cast Unstable Affliction twice, or until you are down to 3 shards on the boss. If you know you will get a larger shard influx due to more Agony agony out on targets, or a large amount of movement comes after your Drain Cycle, cast 3 or 4 times. Any more than 4 is a waste.
  2. Immediately begin casting Drain Soul until all Unstable Affliction have fallen off, or until just before your Agony has fallen off.

Haunt Priority

NOTE: this rotation is mainly for tunneling a single target. It is assumed that you have done some maintenance on dots on various targets before entering this.
  1. Cast Haunt on the priority target. This is ideally a target that will be dying during the duration of the Haunt debuff.
  2. Apply 3-4 Unstable Affliction to that target.
  3. Drain Soul until the target is dead.
  4. Repeat a maintenance cycle and this as priority targets come up.

Multi-Target Rotations

2+ Targets
  1. Apply Agony and maintain it.
  2. Apply Corruption and maintain it. If the targets are clumped, cast Seed of Corruption to spread corruption quicker.
  3. Apply Unstable Affliction if you have 5 shards, try to drop down below 2 shards before continuing. If you specced into Contagion spread these around, otherwise it doesn’t matter who gets these. Never have more than 5 Unstable Affliction on a target at one time.
  4. Apply Siphon Life if specced into it and maintain it.
  5. Cast Summon Doomguard on cooldown.
  6. Cast Grimoire: Felhunter on cooldown.
  7. Cast Life Tap while moving if all instant dots are fully refreshed
  8. Use Drain Soul as your filler on the target that has to die first.
Multi Target Dump Cycles
With your Multi Target Writhe in Agony builds, instead of going through “Drain Cycles” like you do with Malefic Grasp, you will go through Dump Cycles. These are done when you are close to hitting 4-5 Soul Shards to make sure to not waste Soul Shards.
  1. Make sure at least Agony and Corruption are maintained before starting.
  2. If you can, pool your Tormented Souls to at least 4 before starting, but more allows you to just keep going.
  3. Apply Unstable Affliction if you have 5 shards, try to drop down below 2 shards before continuing. If you specced into Contagion spread these around, otherwise it doesn’t matter who gets these. Never have more than 5 Unstable Affliction on a target at one time.
  4. Apply up to 5 Unstable Affliction to one target that will live for the duration of them.
  5. Drain Soul while the Unstable Affliction are ticking, or update other dots like Siphon Life
  6. As the Unstable Affliction fall off, if Reap Souls will last through another application, continue casting Unstable Affliction.
  7. Cast Life Tap while moving if all instant dots are fully refreshed.
  8. Use Drain Soul to snipe targets close to death for Soul Shards.
AoE Fight
  1. Use Seed of Corruption as a filler and apply Corruption with it. When specced into
  2. Cast Summon Infernal if targets are alive for at least 15 seconds.
In AoE-fights you basically spam Seed of Corruption thanks to Sow the Seeds not limiting the amount of targets anymore.

Opening Sequence

With Malefic Grasp:
  1. Use pre-potion on 1.5 seconds
  2. Cast Agony on primary target
  3. Cast Corruption
  4. Cast Siphon Life
  5. Cast Summon Doomguard
  6. Begin going into Drain Cycles
With Writhe in Agony:
  1. Use a potion
  2. Precast Unstable Affliction
  3. Cast Agony
  4. Cast Corruption
  5. Cast Siphon Life
  6. Cast Summon Doomguard if Shard is ready
  7. Cast Grimoire: Felhunter if Shard is ready
  8. Cast Unstable Affliction
  9. Use Drain Soul on the target
  10. Continue regular priority list.
With Haunt
  1. Pre-pot at ~2.5
  2. Pre-cast Haunt at ~2.5
  3. Pre-cast Unstable Affliction at ~1.5
  4. Cast Agony
  5. Cast Corruption
  6. Cast Siphon Life
  7. Add any more Agony, Corruption, and Siphon Life to other targets, using Seed of Corruption if it will save GCDs
  8. Begin using Drain Soul on the haunted target.
Advanced Tips

Pandemic

Maximizing Pandemic and good Reap Souls timing are what make a good Affliction warlock. Pandemic is a passive that all casters now have that allows for 33% of the duration of DoT effects to be added onto new DoTs when they are refreshed. Be aware that refreshing Agony <5.4 seconds remaining is optimal, refreshing Corruption <4.2 seconds remaining is optimal and refreshing Siphon Life <4.5 seconds remaining is optimal.

Reap Souls

Reap Souls should always be used in conjunction with a drain cycle in single target, or in conjunction with a large amount of Agony being stacked to 20 for multi target. For the earlier you want to use it with 2-3 souls, and the later you want to time it to have 4+ souls while all dots are ticking and you can get some Unstable Affliction out as well. If used with a Writhe in Agony spec, your goal is to time it with all your dots up, and time 4 souls with a full five Soul Shards dump of Unstable Affliction

Unstable Affliction

Unstable Affliction Usage: There are currently two methods of how to use Unstable Affliction.
  • In the Drain Cycles described earlier, using 2+ at a time.
  • Without Malefic Grasp your goal is to collect Soul Shards and use all Soul Shards for Unstable Affliction in a row with Reap Souls active. Preferably with Reap Souls and damage beneficial buffs. If you get more Shards while stacking your Unstable Affliction up, you should use them as well. Using Unstable Affliction on short living targets is recommend since you get your Soul Shards within 8 seconds back.
  • Basically each Unstable Affliction counts as an separate debuff but when their duration overlap they do cumulative damage. You do not lose any damage independent when you refresh it.

Conduit vs SL vs DE

With the 7.2.5 change up, we have a few options in the tier 100 tree. The overall play style will change drastically from spec to spec.
  • Siphon Life Gives you an extra dot to maintain, with all the extra damage that comes with it. The damage on this has been improved by a lot.
  • Soul Conduit Adds a lot of RNG to the dump rotation, but will be very strong in conjunction with our new legendary ring:  Soul of the Netherlord. Even without, it will feel very similar to 7.2.5, but will definitely be lower damage.
  • Death's Embrace Doesn’t actually change our rotation from 100-35%, but adds a multiplicative execute on the 35-0% of a fight. Keep in mind to save various CDs such as Soul Harvest for later for maximum deeps.
Additional Thoughts:
  • Try to save Life Tap for use while moving to not lose any dps.
  • It is especially important that you never let fall Agony off your target because you need to rebuild all the stacks.
  • It’s not a big of a deal if Corruption during a drain cycle because there is no damage ramp. Unstable Affliction deals a lot more damage, so you lose more damage stopping to re-apply corruption than you do letting corruption stay off while continuing to drain with Unstable Affliction ticking.

Pet Choice

Due to Grimoire of Supremacy is our go to Grimoire, you will typically be using Summon Doomguard for single target, and Summon Infernal for multi target. Summon Felhunter is our pet choice without Grimoire of Supremacy because it deals more damage per DoT on the target. If you play with Grimoire of Service you also take Grimoire: Felhunter.
