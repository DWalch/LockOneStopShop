Gearing in General

For an auto sorting gearlist please visit this spreadsheet which also includes a handy video presentation on how to use it to find out gearing options in Tomb of Sargeras.

This is a topic that has come up a lot, and I wanted to touch on it. You will notice that our stat weights are SUPER close outside of versatility. This means that gearing up (outside of trinkets) is incredibly easy! You can always run simcraft to figure out if an item will be better, but this basic outline will server you just fine:

  • Single Target: Haste > Mastery > Crit > Vers
  • Two Targets: Mastery > Haste > Crit > Vers
  • General outline: Haste = Mastery > Crit > Vers

Congratulations, if you got a positive result for all 5, this item is an upgrade!

Tier 20

Our new tier 20 is the Diabolic Raiment. The artwork is based on our tier 6: Malefic Raiment, including the famous wings.

  • 2 piece, giving your corruption a 1.2RPPM chance for a soulshard per corruption active.
  • 4 piece, which lets your UA and Seed spells have a chance to proc a 10% haste buff for 8 seconds.

With the loss of Soul Effigy, our shard generation, and therefor burst from UA, has taken a major hit, which means the 2 piece is incredibly important to pick up. The 4 piece is a damage increase for sure, but not nearly as much of one.

Legion-Daries

Another "fun" feature from legion is the fact that everyone gets legendaries. After completing your class hall campaign, you can equip up to 2 at any given time. These legendaries range in power and effect, from having effecting our utilities like Demonic Gateway, to directly increasing our damage from Agony
