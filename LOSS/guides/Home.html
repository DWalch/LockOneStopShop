Welcome to our very own Lock One Stop Shop!

Here you will find guides and sims of every kind for all Warlock specs. On the left will be your navigation guide to each spec with their respective guides and sims included, please use it freely and liberally!

If you find anything to not work, have a question about functionality or have any other questions feel free to contact myself (Not) or anyone else on the LOSS team and they'd be happy to try and answer any questions you might have.
